Our mission

Built by analysts,
for analysts

DataCraft Hub exists because most tutorials teach features, not projects. Every guide here is a complete, real-world build — with files you can use immediately.

What makes this different

Every guide covers the full stack: problem, architecture, data model, code, and deployment. Nothing skipped. All files — Power BI templates, blueprints, cheat sheets — are free.

Who it's for

Working professionals who want to go from "can make reports" to "can architect solutions." And students who want a portfolio they can actually show an employer — not textbook exercises.

Community first

Every guide has open comments — ask questions, share improvements, correct mistakes. Votes surface the best content. The community makes every guide better over time.

What's coming

Sales analytics, Finance dashboards, a complete SQL curriculum, Python for HR data, and eventually a full learning path from zero to deployed analyst dashboard in a day.
